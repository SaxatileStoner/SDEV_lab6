CompSci

My interest in computer science started when I built my first computer. Learning about the hardware of a computer system only allured me further to what lead underneath in terms of software.

What compelled me at a young age to want to take on the task of building a computer? Well, I owe a lot of my computer science intrests to a very popular game at the time called: Minecraft

I know it sounds silly, but a simple game about building anything with blocks compelled me to build a computer to play the game about building anything; a little funny to think of. At the time, it was a PC only title with it being in beta. So, it was my only option, and it didn't need to be the most powerful computer either, minecraft can be run on most things in general (, the beauty of the Java language it was originally written in).

Here are a few of my other favorite games that relate to programming or computer science in some way. Whether the game itself has gameplay elements relating to computer science and programming, or, the way they were programmed is something to admire from a technical standpoint:
